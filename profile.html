About Me

Hey, my name is Clement. I live in Kumasi, Ghana and I am currently building web applications using the Flask framework. My first exposure to web development was in 2020 during the corona virus epidemic when I came across web developement adds whilst surfing Youtube. Fast forward to 2021, I decided to sacrifice a bit of my leisure period to learn this new skill and here I am today. I would like to think that I have learned a lot since then. I am a Python developer and also know C, JavaScript, Bootstrap, CSS and HTML. I can design and manage databases in SQLite, MySQL and PostGreSQL too. Check out some of my work and feel free to send me an email if you would like us to work together.
